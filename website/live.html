About Our Streams

1080p · 30fps

Mevo NDI|HX cameras on every stage, processed through dedicated OBS Studio machines. 6 Mbps CBR — no compression artifacts, no buffering.

Broadcast-Grade Audio

Direct tap from the Behringer X32 mixing console. Dedicated broadcast mix bus per stage at –16 LUFS. The same audio you'd hear at the front of house.

Three Independent Streams

Each stage has its own dedicated streaming PC, camera, and audio feed. Switch between streams independently. Nothing shares bandwidth.
