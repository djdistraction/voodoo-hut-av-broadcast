The Origin

How a Bus Became a Bar

Not everything at The VooDoo Hut was planned. The bus wasn't a concept — it was a feeling. Someone looked at an old vehicle sitting somewhere it didn't belong and saw exactly where it did belong: parked inside 14,000 square feet of controlled chaos, converted into a bar, serving drinks to people who didn't know they needed to find it.

That's been the Hut's whole philosophy since day one. You walk in, and there's more than you expected. Then more. Then something you can't explain. The bus is just the most literal version of that — a door inside a door, a bar inside a bar.

If you've never had a drink on The Bus, put it on the list. It hits different.
